HMS Scrap

Our HMS (Heavy Melting Scrap) is a high-grade metal scrap consisting primarily of heavy steel and iron materials, often sourced from demolished structures, obsolete machinery, and industrial equipment. This robust scrap is ideal for recycling and repurposing into new steel and iron products due to its substantial metal content and durability. HMS Scrap offers a cost-effective and sustainable solution for manufacturers and steel producers, helping to reduce raw material costs while promoting environmental responsibility. By choosing our HMS Scrap, you contribute to a more circular economy and efficient resource use, supporting high-quality production while minimizing waste.
